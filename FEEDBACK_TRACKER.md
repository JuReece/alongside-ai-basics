# Feedback Tracker

**Purpose:** Track feedback from Arketeers team and prioritize actions
**Date Started:** May 2024

---

## 📝 How to Use This Document

1. **Record feedback** as it comes in under the appropriate category
2. **Add date and person** who gave the feedback
3. **Note priority** (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
4. **Track status** (New, Discussing, Planned, In Progress, Done)
5. **Update regularly** as decisions are made

---

## 🎯 Feedback Categories

### Content & Educational Approach
Feedback about lessons, prompts, resources, and teaching methodology

### User Experience & Design
Feedback about interface, navigation, visual design, and usability

### Technical & Features
Feedback about functionality, performance, and technical implementation

### Strategy & Vision
Feedback about overall direction, fit with AI Ark mission, and big picture
