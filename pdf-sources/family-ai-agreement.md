*Our Guide to Using AI Safely and Responsibly Together*

## Introduction

This agreement helps our family use AI in ways that are safe, honest, and helpful. We created this together and agree to follow these guidelines.

## Our Family's AI Values

We believe AI should be used to:
- ✅ Learn and grow
- ✅ Solve problems creatively
- ✅ Save time on routine tasks
- ✅ Support each other
- ✅ Enhance our abilities

We agree AI should NOT be used to:
- ❌ Cheat or deceive
- ❌ Replace thinking
- ❌ Harm or bully others
- ❌ Share private information
- ❌ Avoid responsibility

---

## Privacy & Safety Rules

### What We Will NEVER Share with AI:

□ Full names with location details
□ Complete home address
□ Phone numbers
□ Email addresses (with identifying info)
□ Passwords or PINs
□ Bank or credit card details
□ National Insurance/Social Security numbers
□ Medical record numbers
□ Photos showing faces or identifiable features
□ School names with personal details
□ Exact locations or routines

### How We'll Protect Privacy:

□ Use general terms instead of specific details
□ Remove identifying information before sharing
□ Think before we type
□ Ask a parent/guardian if unsure

**Example:**
- ❌ "My son Jake, age 7, at Riverside Primary..."
- ✅ "My 7-year-old is learning..."

---

## Homework & School Work

### We Agree To:

□ **Use AI to LEARN**, not to cheat
□ **Ask AI to explain**, not do our work
□ **Write in our own words**
□ **Show our working and thinking**
□ **Tell teachers when we use AI** (if required)
□ **Never copy-paste** AI responses as our own work

### For Parents/Guardians:

□ Help children use AI appropriately
□ Supervise younger children's AI use
□ Check that children understand their work
□ Communicate with teachers about AI use
□ Teach the difference between help and cheating

### For Children/Students:

□ Only use AI in ways my school allows
□ Ask for help if I don't understand something
□ Do my own thinking and writing
□ Be honest about using AI
□ Come to parents if I feel pressured to cheat

---

## Age-Appropriate Use

### For Children Under 13:

□ Only use AI with parent/guardian present
□ Parent/guardian reads all prompts and responses
□ Stick to approved activities:
  - Learning explanations
  - Creative stories
  - Age-appropriate questions
□ No chatting as if AI is a friend
□ Regular breaks from screen time

---

### For Ages 13-16:

□ Use AI with parent/guardian nearby
□ Regular check-ins about what I'm doing
□ Follow all safety and privacy rules
□ No using AI for inappropriate content
□ No pretending AI-generated work is mine
□ Come to parents with concerns or questions

---

### For Ages 16+:

□ Use AI responsibly and ethically
□ Follow academic integrity rules
□ Protect privacy (mine and others')
□ Think critically about AI responses
□ Help younger siblings use AI safely
□ Discuss AI use openly with family

## NOT Approved - We Will NOT Use AI For:

### ❌ Prohibited Uses:

□ Cheating on homework, tests, or assignments
□ Creating fake identities or catfishing

## Fact-Checking & Critical Thinking

### We Agree To:

□ **Question everything** AI tells us
□ **Verify important information** from trusted sources
□ **Use multiple sources** for research
□ **Think critically** about AI responses
□ **Recognize AI can make mistakes**
□ **Not believe something** just because AI said it

### Our Fact-Checking Process:

1. AI gives us information
2. We ask: "Does this make sense?"
3. We check 2-3 other reliable sources
4. We discuss as a family if unsure
5. We use human experts for important decisions

## Communication & Openness

### We Promise To:

□ **Talk openly** about AI use in our family
□ **Ask questions** when we're unsure
□ **Admit mistakes** without fear of harsh punishment
□ **Share concerns** about AI safety or misuse
□ **Listen to each other's** perspectives
□ **Update this agreement** as we learn more

### No Judgement Zone:

If someone makes a mistake with AI use, we will:
1. Talk about it calmly
2. Understand why it happened
3. Learn from it together
4. Adjust our rules if needed
5. Move forward positively

## Consequences & Accountability

### If Someone Breaks This Agreement:

**First Time:**
1. Calm conversation about what happened
2. Discussion of why the rule exists
3. Reminder of expectations
4. No consequences if honest and learning

**Repeated Issues:**
1. Temporary loss of AI access
2. Increased supervision
3. Required check-ins
4. Work together to solve underlying issue

**Serious Violations** (cheating, sharing private info, bullying):
1. Immediate loss of AI privileges
2. Family meeting to discuss
3. Natural consequences (e.g., school penalties for cheating)
4. Plan for earning back trust
5. Professional help if needed

---

## Special Situations

### When Someone Feels Uncomfortable:

If AI responses make anyone uncomfortable:
□ Stop immediately
□ Tell a parent/guardian
□ Discuss what happened
□ Block/report if appropriate
□ Learn from the experience

---

### If We See Others Misusing AI:

□ Don't participate or encourage it
□ Gently suggest better behavior
□ Tell a trusted adult if serious
□ Remember: we're not tattling, we're helping

---

### If AI Gives Concerning Information:

□ Show a parent/guardian immediately
□ Don't follow questionable advice
□ Discuss as a family
□ Report to platform if needed
